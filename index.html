VisionOCR

Concept:

A smart scanning tool designed to automate the manual process of entering receipts and invoices. By simply uploading or taking a photo, VisionOCR extracts key details such as vendors, dates, items and totals with high accuracy. The system organizes each entry into clear structured data. This provides easy tracking spending, managing and staying on top of monthly budgets without manual input. Everyday users can rely on VisionOCR for groceries, budgeting and building healthier spending habits. In the future, all processing will run locally to ensure complete privacy and security of your data.

TCM Helper

Concept:

Growing up Asian-American, I have vivid memories of my parents making us drink bitter soups and teas. I did not understand why at the time, but with so much information available today and after reading more studies on Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), I have realized they were trying to keep us healthy and not punish us. While there is a long-standing debate between Eastern and Western medicine, TCM often faces skepticism about its effectiveness. The TCM Helper is designed to educate people on TCM ingredients and formulas, highlighting which are supported by research and which are best avoided. It will also provide simple daily recipes tailored to specific health concerns and feature an interactive drag-and-drop interface where users can mix ingredients and instantly see the potential benefits and side effects of each combination.
